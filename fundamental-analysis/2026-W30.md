This scan is scored under the **relative rule (line 217)** — the one written into the entry gate — for consistency with W28. Currencies whose classification flips between the strict and relative rules are marked **⚖️**. For those, the raw 🟢/🔴 count is shown so you can re-score under whichever rule you settle on.

Two currencies also carry a **policy override** that the mechanical count misses:
- **CHF** — mechanically 6🟢, but the SNB has stated it will *sell CHF into strength*. Un-tradeable as a long leg regardless of the score.
- **HUF** — count is 3🟢 vs 2🔴, but the two reds are an active **cutting cycle** + below-band CPI, which are the highest-signal indicators for FX direction. Classified bearish on policy, against the count.
