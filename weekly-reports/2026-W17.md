Iran-driven energy price pressures are expected to push core inflation toward 3% by end-FY2026. The BOJ is expected to cut its FY2026 growth forecast and revise its inflation forecast upward at its April 27–28 meeting.

### NZD — New Zealand Dollar

### CAD — Canadian Dollar

March CPI climbed to 2.4% YoY (from 1.8%), propelled by a 21% surge in gasoline prices linked to Middle East tensions. The CAD held up better than most G10 currencies given the oil price tailwind.

### MXN — Mexican Peso
