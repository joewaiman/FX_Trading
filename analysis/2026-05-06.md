# FX Fundamental Analysis — 2026-05-06

## Context

Global backdrop shaped by the **Iran conflict** driving elevated energy prices, creating stagflation dynamics in energy-importing economies and a terms-of-trade boost for commodity exporters (AUD, CAD, NZD). BoJ is in a gradual hiking cycle, RBA just hiked to 4.35%, and the ECB has pivoted hawkish on inflation. The Fed remains on hold for a third consecutive meeting with leadership transition underway (Powell term ends May 15).
